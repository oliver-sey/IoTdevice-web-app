About the Project:

The Heart-Rate Helper application is a low-cost IoT-enabled web app for monitoring heart rate and blood oxygen saturation level throughout the day. In this project, we used Particle IoT board (Argon) and Heart Rate Sensor (Module MAX30102 Pulse Detection Blood Oxygen) as our hardware devices. We used AWS EC2 instance as our cloud application server, Express.js and MongoDB (mongoose) for our backend services and server-side APIs, and HTML/CSS/Javascript/jQuery/Ajax for our frontend services and client-side APIs.
